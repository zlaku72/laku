Research and Projects

Here are some notable academic project and research works of mine:

  • Immense Fidelity Enhancement of Encoded Quantum Bell Pairs at Short and Long-distance Communication along with Generalized Design of Circuit

    Submitted article. arXiv preprint: arXiv:2303.07425v1 [quant-ph]

  • Effect of Quantum Repetition Code on Fidelity of Bell States in Bit Flip Channels

    This paper is based on my undergraduate thesis, supervised by Dr. Md. Saifur Rahman, Professor, Dept. of EEE, BUET), Accepted in 12th International Conference on Electrical and Computer Engineering (ICECE). Researchgate preprint is available here.

  • Effect of EOG Artifact Removal on EEG Motor-Imagery Classification

    This paper is based on our Biomedical Engineering sessional course project during L4T2, accepted in 12th International Conference on Electrical and Computer Engineering (ICECE). DOI link https://doi.org/10.1109/ICCIT57492.2022.10056062.

  • Image Encoding and Decoding using FRQI and NEQR Method using Qiskit

    This was an assignment project during my RA-ship. Link of jupyter notebook: here.

  • Load Flow Analysis for n Bus using Gauss-Siedel Algorithm :

    It was our sessional course project for Power System II during L3T2. I used Microsoft Excel for input and output data, and MATLAB to generalize the algorithm and generate output. Github Link: https://github.com/syedshubha/load_flow_analysis_gauss_seidel

  • Image Encryption using XOR operation and Random Permutation Generation from password:

    It was our Numerical Techniques Sessional Course project during L2T1. MATLAB was used for this project. Github Link: https://github.com/syedshubha/ImageEncryption

  • Bangla Speech Emotion Detection:

    Using Machine Learning, four Bengali speech emotions were classified: Happy, Sad, Angry and Neutral. This was our course project for DSP-I during L3T1.

Hobbies and Achievements

In my free time, I like to write and read about Physics and Mathematics. Although I am from Engineering background, I always have been fascinated by Mathematics and Physics. I try to learn Physic and Mathematics from textbooks, which are not included in Engineering curriculum. I invested a fair amount of time to learn many concepts taught in Physics and Mathematics curriculum.

I also connected with several science organizations during the freshman year of my undergraduate studies. I love to write about science to make more people interested in science. I believe, if mass people understand how scientific principles work and act on it in their real life, then we can altogether make the world a better place. So I write scientific blogs and sometimes make youtube videos explaining some nice concepts. I also believe that without Mathematics, realization of science is incomplete. So I emphasize on math teaching in both high school and varsity level. I personally emphasize on mathematics and teach myself as much as possible. Feynman once said, "If you want to master something, teach it". I heartly follow this in my life.

Aside from Physics and Mathematics, I am interested in History, Geopolitics, etc. I love animals and I believe in "Live and let live". I make memes and play chess in my free time. I always appreciate humor and chess knowledge. I am still novice at chess (learning for about a year). If you want to play with me, you can ping me on Lichess or Chess.com.
