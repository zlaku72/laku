Current Research

  • Quantum Chaos, Information Scrambling and OTOC: Currently studying thesis papers, Chaos and Randomness in Strongly-Interacting Quantum Systems by Nicholas R. Hunter-Jones and Quantum steampunk: Quantum information, thermodynamics, their intersection, and applications thereof across physics by Nicole Yunger Halpern
  • Quantum Surface and Toric Code: Currently studies articles from several resources.
  • Quantum Image Processing, Compression and Edge Detection: We already modified QHED for better edge detection. Currently we are trying to modify Sobel mask for edge detection, based on NEQR.
  • GHZ based Quantum Communication and Security: We made some progress on GHZ based teleportation and partial error correction. Recently we are studying QKD protocol based on GHZ state and modified CHSH inequality.
  • Quantum Energy Teleportation: Currently studying journal articles on this topic.
